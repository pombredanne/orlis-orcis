Simple Weather Forecast gives you a 14-day forecast based on your current location. This application is extremely light, fast, free and without any ads.
It doesn't collect any personal information.

<b>Permissions</b>
&rarr; Approximate location: Simple Weather Forecast uses your approximate location to give you a relevant forecast.
&rarr; Network access and connections: Simple Weather Forecast uses the network only to get the forecast from openweathermap, and that's it.
&rarr; Google Play billing service : This is only to offer the possibility of making donations to support our open-source developments. 

<b>See for yourself!</b>
Simple Weather Forecast is an open source application, it can't hide anything from you! You can explore its functioning at the following link https://github.com/tvbarthel/SimpleWeatherForecast
